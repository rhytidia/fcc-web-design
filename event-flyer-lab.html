Block Party!

Celebrate the Summer Soltice with your neighbours

The Summer Solstice is coming up, and it's time to get together for food, fun, and community as we celebrate the longest day of the year.

Bring a dish to share and your own utensils. There will be some snacks and drinks generously donated by local businesses.

There will also be games for all ages, so come prepared to play!

When and Where

Sunday, June 21: Drop by anytime between 12pm-6pm.

Find us: On the boulevard between 1st and 2nd streets.
